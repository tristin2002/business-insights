About Raj Ladies Tailor

The Journey of Raj Shihora

Welcome to Raj Ladies Tailor, where passion for fashion meets unparalleled craftsmanship. Founded by Raj Shihora, our boutique has been serving the women of Ahmedabad with exquisite tailoring services that blend traditional techniques with contemporary style.

Our Story

With over 15 years of experience in women's fashion and tailoring, Raj Shihora started this boutique with a vision to create clothing that makes every woman feel confident, elegant, and beautiful. What began as a small tailoring shop has grown into Ahmedabad's trusted destination for premium women's ethnic wear and bridal fashion.

Our expertise lies in understanding the unique needs of each client and translating their vision into perfectly crafted garments. From intricate bridal lehengas to everyday ethnic wear, we pay attention to every detail, ensuring that each piece reflects our commitment to quality and elegance.
