Our Story

Meet Raj Shihora

With a passion for fashion and an eye for detail, Raj Shihora established Raj Ladies Tailor with a vision to bring boutique-quality tailoring to every woman in Ahmedabad. Our journey began with a simple belief - every woman deserves to wear clothes that make her feel confident and beautiful.

Today, we're proud to be the trusted choice for brides, fashion enthusiasts, and women who value quality craftsmanship. Each garment we create tells a story of elegance, tradition, and modern sophistication.
