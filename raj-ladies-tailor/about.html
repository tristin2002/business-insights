Meet Raj Shihora

With years of dedicated experience in women's tailoring, Raj brings couture sensibilities to everyday and bridal wear. Each garment is thoughtfully cut, meticulously fitted, and finished with boutique-level detailing.

From timeless saree blouses and kurtis to luxurious bridal lehengas and gowns, we tailor pieces that feel as good as they look. Our approach is feminine, refined, and focused on delivering a comfortable, confident fit.

We believe in clear communication, precise measurements, and premium craftsmanship—values our clients appreciate and return for.
